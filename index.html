Julia Heemskerk
software developer | UX/UI design

Hello !

I’m Julia, a software development student from The Netherlands with a passion for creating clean, user-friendly websites. I first got into web development when my dad introduced me to Swift Playground, where I had a lot of fun playing around with while-loops and if-statements. I also spend some time playing around on Codecademy. Even when family members had questions about something not working on their laptop or phone, I was often the first person they came to for help.


What I really like about software development is that a software developer can look at a wireframe or mock-up, and based on that can make ideas really come to life. When someone needs adjustments on their websites or has a certain question, they can come to you and you can help them solve their problem. At the moment I’m focused on improving my coding knowledge and am focused on learning back-end and implementing that into front-end.
